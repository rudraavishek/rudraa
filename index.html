Avishek Rudra

Welcome to my homepage! I am a Kolkatan currently living and working in Cambridge, UK.

I am a Geochemist striving to understand planetary processes through experiments and codes. I use high pressure temperature experiments to understand chemical mass transfer between Earth's mantle and surface. This includes cycling of volatile elements (H, C, N, S, F and noble gases) and oxidized basaltic component between the mantle and exosphere and how these fluxes have evolved over geologic time.
